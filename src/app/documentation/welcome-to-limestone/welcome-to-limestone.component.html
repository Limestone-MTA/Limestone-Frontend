What is Limestone?

Limestone is a comprehensive statistical analysis platform that simplifies complex data analysis, offering a wide array of quality control, visualizations, and statistical tests.

  • Intuitive Interface: Perform powerful analyses with just a few clicks, no coding required.
  • Extensive Statistical Methods: Access a wide range of statistical tests, including linear and logistic regression.
  • Interactive Visualizations: Generate dynamic charts and plots that help you understand your data effectively.
